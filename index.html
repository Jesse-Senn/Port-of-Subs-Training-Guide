Hot Sandwich Make Order:

  1. All-American Hot Sub:
    • Ingredients: Cheese, ham, turkey, bacon.
    • Turbo chef instructions: Place all ingredients on top of the bread. Grill twice for 45 seconds. Place on foil.
    • Grill press instructions: Put bacon on top of the cheese. Grill twice for 45 seconds. Place on foil.
  2. Gourmet Grilled Cheese:
    • Ingredients: Provolone, Swiss, smokey cheddar, pepper jack.
    • Turbo chef instructions: Grill twice in the grill press.
    • Grill press instructions: Grill twice in the grill press.
  3. Ultimate BLT:
    • Ingredients: Provolone, bacon.
    • Grill instructions: Place provolone on the bread, add bacon on top. Grill once. Fill the wrap.
  4. Meatball:
    • Ingredients: Provolone, meatball.
    • Microwave instructions: Heat the meatball for 1-1.5 minutes in the microwave.
    • Turbo chef instructions: Put the meatball on the bottom. Grill once for 45 seconds.
  5. Grilled Chicken Melt - 4 ways (Plain, Buffalo, BBQ, Teriyaki):
    • Ingredients: Microwave chicken, provolone.
    • Microwave instructions: Microwave the chicken for about 1 minute.
    • Turbo chef instructions: Put the cheese on top if using Turbo chef. Otherwise, put the cooked chicken on top of the cheese.
    • Grill instructions: Grill for 45 seconds. Add 2 scoops of teriyaki, BBQ, or buffalo sauce. Shake and place on top of the cheese. Wrap in foil.
  6. Pulled Pork Melt - 2 ways (Plain or BBQ):
    • Ingredients: Cheese, microwave pork.
    • Microwave instructions: Microwave the pork.
    • Turbo chef instructions: Put the pork on the bread with cheese on top.
    • Grill press instructions: Make it normally. Wrap in foil.
  7. Got Pastrami Melt:
    • Ingredients: Cheese, microwave pastrami.
    • Turbo chef instructions: Place the pastrami on the bread if using Turbo chef.
    • Grill press instructions: Make it normally. Grill once.
    • Microwave instructions: Microwave the pastrami portion for 1 minute.
  8. NY Steak Melt:
    • Ingredients: Cheese, microwave steak.
    • Microwave instructions: Microwave the steak portion for 1 minute.
    • Grill instructions: Grill once for 45 seconds. Wrap in foil.
